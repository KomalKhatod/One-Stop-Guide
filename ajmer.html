Welcome to Ajmer

Nestled in the heart of Rajasthan, Ajmer is a city steeped in spiritual heritage and glorious Mughal-Rajput history. From the serene Ana Sagar Lake to the legendary Dargah Sharif and the scenic Aravalli hills, explore Ajmer's rich tapestry of culture, architecture, and cuisine.

Ajmer’s golden domes, marble pavilions, and bustling bazaars invite you to a cultural experience like no other. Visit Dargah Sharif, the Sufi shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti, and wander through the mystical lanes of old Ajmer. Stroll along Ana Sagar Lake at sunset, take in the architectural wonder of Adhai-din-ka-Jhonpra, and trek up to Taragarh Fort for panoramic views of the city. Don’t miss the colorful festivals, delectable Rajasthani thali, and the unique blend of Mughal-Rajput charm!

Ajmer also serves as a gateway to Pushkar, known for its sacred lake and Brahma temple. The city blends the historical with the modern, making it a vibrant place to visit and explore.
