Welcome to Fatehpur

Fatehpur is a city that reflects the grandeur of Mughal architecture and rich cultural heritage. From the majestic Fatehpur Sikri to the serene banks of the Yamuna, this city offers a glimpse into India's glorious past. Fatehpur is known for its rich historical background, beautiful architecture, and its location along the banks of the Yamuna River. It is a city with a blend of cultural heritage and natural beauty.

Fatehpur, located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, is a city steeped in history and culture. It was founded by Mughal Emperor Akbar in the 16th century and served as the capital of the Mughal Empire for a brief period. The city is renowned for its stunning Mughal architecture, including palaces, mosques, and forts. Fatehpur hosts many heritage sites such as the Fatehpur Sikri, Jama Masjid, and the Buland Darwaza. It is also famous for its local crafts and vibrant festivals.


Things to do:

  • Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Fatehpur Sikri
  • Explore the majestic Buland Darwaza
  • Discover the intricate architecture of Jama Masjid
  • Experience local crafts and markets
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty along the Yamuna River
