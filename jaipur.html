Welcome to Jaipur

Jaipur is a vibrant city known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and colorful culture. As the capital of Rajasthan, it is often referred to as the "Pink City" due to the distinct color of its buildings.

Jaipur, founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city is famous for its palaces, forts, and vibrant bazaars. Key attractions include the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Amber Fort. Jaipur is also known for its handicrafts, textiles, and traditional Rajasthani cuisine.


Things to do:

  • Visit the iconic Hawa Mahal and explore its intricate architecture.
  • Discover the history at the City Palace and Jantar Mantar observatory.
  • Experience the grandeur of Amber Fort with its stunning views.
  • Shop for traditional handicrafts and textiles in the local bazaars.
  • Savor authentic Rajasthani cuisine at local eateries.
